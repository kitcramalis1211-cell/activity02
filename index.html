Cinnamon Rolls

Ingredients

for the dough:

  • ¾ cup warm milk
  • 2 ¼ teaspoons yeast
  • ¼ cup granulated sugar
  • 1 Egg plus 1 egg yolk
  • ¼ Cup butter
  • 3 Cups bread flour

For the filling

  • 2/cup dark brown sugar
  • 1 ½ tablespoons ground cinnamon
  • ¼ Cup butter

Instruction

  1. Mix the milk with the yest, sugar,eggs.
  2. Melt the butter and add to the mixture.
  3. Add in the flour and mix until combined into a dough.
  4. Knead the dough for 10minutes.
  5. Transfer the dough into a large bowl cover with plastic wrap leave
    it somewhere to rise for 2 hours.
  6. After the dough has double in size, roll it out inyo a large rectangle.
  7. Melt the butter for the filling and mix in the sugar and cinnamon.
  8. Spread the filling onto the dough then roll the dough into a swiss roll
  9. Cut the roll into 3cm section and place flat into a baking tray.
  10. Pre-heat the oven to 350F or 180c, then bake the rolls for 20-25min until lightly brown.
